Maintenance Practices for Vacuum Cleaner Filter
Clean Regularly: Wash the filter every 2–4 weeks to prevent dust buildup and maintain strong suction.
Dry Completely: Always air-dry the filter fully before reinstalling to avoid moisture damage.
Inspect for Wear: Check for tears or clogging that may reduce filtration efficiency.
Replace on Time: Change the filter every 6–12 months for optimal performance.
Avoid Chemicals: Clean using water only; detergents can damage the filter material.
