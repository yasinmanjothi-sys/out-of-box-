Key Features and Benefits
Easy Attachment: Quickly swap between grits without adhesive mess, making them ideal for progressive sanding.
Versatility: Available inVarious sizes, commonly 5-inch (125mm) or 6-inch (150mm) for random orbit sanders, as well as specialized shapes like triangular, rectangular, and "mouse" shapes for corner sanding.
Dust Extraction: Many pads come pre-punched with holes (e.g., 8-hole or 15-hole patterns) to allow debris to pass through, reducing clogging and improving air quality.
Material: Often constructed from durable aluminum oxide, suitable for wood, metal, plastic, and filler materials.
Cost-Effective: Reusable design allows for long-term use, and worn-out Velcro backing pads on tools can be easily replaced. 
Usage Tips
Compatibility: Ensure the sanding discs match the hole pattern of your sander for maximum dust collection.
Replacement: To replace a worn-out velcro base on a sander, clean the pad thoroughly with alcohol, remove old debris, and ensure a 24-hour curing time for new adhesive-backed Velcro.
Storage: Avoid storing sanders face-down to prevent the hook fasteners from bending, which weakens their grip. 
Velcro sanding pads are highly recommended for both DIY enthusiasts and professionals for tasks requiring fast, clean, and consistent finishes
