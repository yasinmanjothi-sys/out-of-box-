The Dayliff DDC centrifugal non self priming closed impeller pump is specially designed for all small scale domestic and commercial water supply uses and is particularly suited for water boosting and transfer applications. The pump body is cast iron and the impeller brass.

DDC158 Econo is of lighter construction and is fitted with stainless steel impeller.

MOTORS

Pumps are close coupled to reliable TEFC electric motors designed for continuous operation and are provided with a thermal cutout to protect against electrical overload. They can be connected directly to the mains power supply through a 10A fuse or MCB.

Enclosure Class: IP44
Insulation Class: F
Speed: 2900rpm
